Cereal

The following recipe is how to make a bowl of cereal

Ingredients

    bowl
    Pebbles or off-brand if you're strapped for cash
    spoon
    whole milk or alternative if you're feeling husky

Steps

    find bowl in pile of dishes
    add cereal to bowl
    add milk to bowl (these steps can be switched if you're a spawn of satan)
    put spoon in bowl
    put spoon in mouth
